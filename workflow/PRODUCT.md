# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Nguoi dung chinh la nhan vien noi bo trong doanh nghiep. Ho su dung he thong de thuc hien cac nghiep vu van phong hang ngay: quan ly tai lieu, gui va theo doi don tu, xu ly cong viec, trao doi noi bo, dang ky tai san, tra cuu thong bao va lam viec voi cac luong phe duyet.

He thong cung phuc vu cac vai tro phan cap khac: leader va cac bo phan phu trach xu ly nghiep vu theo tung chuc nang; cap sep hoac nguoi co tham quyen phe duyet cac luong quan trong; admin quan tri nguoi dung, danh muc, audit log va cau hinh he thong.

## Product Purpose

Day la he thong E-Office quan ly tai lieu va nghiep vu noi bo, gom cac chuc nang ho tro nhan vien vao mot noi. Muc tieu la giup nhan vien thuc hien cong viec hanh chinh, xu ly van ban, quan ly task, theo doi quy trinh va nhan phan hoi phe duyet mot cach ro rang, co phan cap va de kiem soat.

Uu tien trien khai truoc la giao dien web va dua he thong len cloud server de co the van hanh, kiem thu va su dung tap trung. Khong co muc tieu phat trien native iOS hoac Android trong giai do hien tai; mobile chi can duoc xem nhu responsive web neu can.

## Positioning

Mot cong E-Office thong nhat, phan cap theo vai tro va phong ban, giup nhan vien xu ly tai lieu, cong viec va quy trinh phe duyet ma khong phai chuyen qua nhieu he thong roi rac.

## Brand Personality

Giao dien can chuyen nghiep va ro rang. Cam giac tong the nen dang tin cay, co trat tu, tap trung vao nghiep vu va giup nguoi dung hieu ngay minh can lam gi tiep theo.

## Anti-references

Khong di theo huong mau me, qua nhieu hieu ung, qua roi mat, hoac giong landing page marketing. Tranh cac bo cuc trang tri lam lu mo tac vu, cac mau sac day dac khong co vai tro, va cac man hinh phai doc qua nhieu moi hieu duoc hanh dong chinh.

## Design Principles

1. Phan cap ro rang theo vai tro: nhan vien, leader, bo phan phu trach, cap phe duyet va admin phai thay dung chuc nang cua minh ma khong bi nhiu boi cac quyen khong lien quan.
2. Uu tien tac vu nghiep vu hon trang tri: moi man hinh can lam noi bat viec can xu ly, trang thai hien tai, nguoi phu trach va hanh dong tiep theo.
3. Mot noi de theo doi dong cong viec: tai lieu, don tu, task, thong bao, tai san va phe duyet nen co cach dieu huong va ngon ngu trang thai nhat quan.
4. Minh bach va de kiem soat: cac hanh dong quan trong can co phan hoi ro, audit trail, trang thai loi de hieu, va du thong tin de nguoi dung tin vao quy trinh.
5. Web-first, cloud-ready: thiet ke truoc cho su dung web tren may tinh va trinh duyet hien dai, dong thoi giu bo cuc du linh hoat de su dung duoc tren man hinh nho khi can.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Yeu cau accessibility o muc Strict / Advanced. Giao dien can de doc, co tuong phan tot, ho tro dieu huong ban phim, focus state ro rang, nhan biet trang thai khong chi dua vao mau, va ton trong tuy chon giam chuyen dong. Cac luong nghiep vu quan trong can co thong bao loi, thanh cong va trang thai cho ro rang de nhan vien khong thao tac nham.
